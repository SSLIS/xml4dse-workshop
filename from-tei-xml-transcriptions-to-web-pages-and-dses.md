The aim of this video lecture was to demonstrate how we can associate XSLT transformation scenarios to our TEI-XML transcriptions to turn them into various new HTML pages using oXygen -- to build a website or Digital Scholarly Edition. Before we get there, however, we have a closer look at how we might simply visualise our XML pages by using CSS (just as we might a regular HTML page), and discover why we would go through the trouble of transforming our XML into HTML in the first place.
