In this video, we will have a quick introduction to the technological foundations of the Internet, to learn how we can host documents and information on the World Wide Web -- and what the difference between the Web and the Internet is, exactly.

This video was made for the Datalogical Thinking course, specifically its Module called: 'Sharing the Knowledge'. A large part of the video was directly copied from a video Wout originally made for a course called 'Technologies for Digital Libraries 1' at the University of Borås. Some voice over and slide images were added to contextualise the lecture in the Datalogical Thinking course.
