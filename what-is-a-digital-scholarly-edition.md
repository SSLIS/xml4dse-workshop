This video offers a quick introduction to the field of Digital Scholarly Editing, situating it within the field of Textual Criticism. The second half of the lecture gives a quick demonstration of a Digital Scholarly Edition by highlighting some key features of the [Beckett Digital Manuscript Project](https://www.beckettarchive.org), and concludes by briefly relating this subject to the field of Library and Information Science.
