# About this workshop

These pages contain the training materials for a full-day modular and reusable workshop that teaches students basic technologies for Digital Scholarly Editing (TEI-XML, HTML, CSS) from scratch. It is specifically designed to help teachers include an introduction to (TEI-)XML in courses on Digital Text Analysis, which often mostly focus on introducing students to quantitative approaches to the subject, such as topic modelling.

The workshop was originally designed by [Wout Dillen](https://orcid.org/0000-0002-7862-2403) as a guest-workshop in such a course on Digital Text Analysis, taught in the [University of Uppsala](https://www.uu.se)'s MA programme in Digital Humanities. It builds heavily on a similar, shorter workshop Wout designed in a standalone course on 'Digitala Metoder' [Digital (Research) Methods)], that was taught at the [University of Borås](https://www.hb.se), and puts together a series of materials Wout previously developed for his own classes in various courses across MA programmes in Borås.
