# About this workshop

These pages contain the training materials for a full-day workshop teaching students basic technologies for Digital Scholarly Editing (TEI-XML, HTML, CSS) from scratch. The workshop was designed by [Wout Dillen](https://orcid.org/0000-0002-7862-2403) for a class in a course on Digital Text Analys taught in the University of Uppsala's MA programme in Digital Humanities. It puts together a series of materials Wout previously developed for his own classes in various courses across MA programmes at the University of Borås.
